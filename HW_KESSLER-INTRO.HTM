Questions for pp. 1-10 in Kessler
 

(1) What are the definitions of the following terms: (a) philosophy, (b) metaphysics, (c) epistemology, (d) axiology, and (e) the Law of Noncontradiction?

(2) What is the difference between foundationalism and anti-foundationalism?

(3) Why say that the cognitive and ethical relativism of anti-foundationalism is self-defeating?

(4) The alternatives of foundationalism and anti-foundationalism are characterized by Kessler as a dilemma---why?

(5) What are the possible responses to this dilemma, and what response does Kessler recommend?
