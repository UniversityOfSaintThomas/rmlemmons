Society of Thomistic Personalism

A fellowship interested in the Thomistic Personalism proposed by Karol Wojtyla

We meet annually as a satellite session at the American Catholic Philosophical Society.                        

Our Call for Papers for our 2010 Session focuses on any connection with Wojtyla's Thomistic Personalism ranging from metaphysics to ethics.

Deadline for conference papers is July 31st.

 
 

For more information about the Society, membership, or e-journal submissions contact Mary Lemmons at  rmlemmons@stthomas.edu
