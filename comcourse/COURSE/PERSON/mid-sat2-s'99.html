Answer ANY two of the following questions:
 

1: Of the philosophers whom we have studied, which two do you to be the most opposed on the question of what constitutes this life=s meaning. Justify your answer.

2: Of the philosophers whom we have studied, which two do you consider to be in the greatest degree of harmony? Justify your answer.

3: Aristotle argued that happiness is possible in this life because it is possible to act virtuously. If Aristotle were to meet Buddha, what would be the points of agreement and disagreement? Justify your answer.

4: Plato argued against the materialism of his age that human beings have immaterial souls. What is his strongest argument? Justify your answer.

5. Plato argued that the human soul never perishes, what is his strongest argument? Justify your answer.
