The idea of reincarnation influenced Indian--but not  ancient Chinese- beliefs. As a result, while the Chinese focused upon establishing either a harmonious society   (Confucian thought) or an immortal physical existence   (Taoism), the Indians focused on ending the cycle of  rebirth.
