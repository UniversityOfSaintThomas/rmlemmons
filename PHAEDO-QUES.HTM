ANALYZING THE PHAEDO



1. THE ARGUMENT AGAINST SUICIDE: Why is suicide immoral?



2a: THE ARGUMENT AGAINST PHILOSOPHER'S FEARING DEATH: why shouldn't philosophers fear death? 2b: Should others fear death? (Hints: What are ghosts? How do the various types of souls reincarnate? How is the body a prison for the soul? What are the nails that rivet the soul to the body?)



3. THE ARGUMENT THAT THE ABSOLUTE FORMS OF JUSTICE, BEAUTY AND EQUALITY MUST EXIST: why must these forms truly exist? (Hint: these forms cannot be perceived by the senses: why not? Is the body helpful in any kind of knowing? Is any essence perceivable?)



4. THE ARGUMENT AGAINST IDENTIFYING THE SOUL AS THE HARMONY OF THE BODY: why cannot the soul be the body's harmony?



5: THE ARGUMENT AGAINST THE SOUL'S MORTALITY: why must the soul never perish regardless of how many reincarnations it endures?
