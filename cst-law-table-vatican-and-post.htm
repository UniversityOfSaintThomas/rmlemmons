Documents of Vatican Council II. Web page. Available at

Abstract: This web site provides access to the documents generated by the Second Vatican Council (1962-65).

 

Vatican Council II. "Gaudium et Spes." Web page. Available at

http://www.vatican.va/archive/hist_councils/ii_vatican_council/documents/vat-ii_cons_19651207_gaudium-et-spes_en.html.

Abstract: A seminal document of the Second Vatican Council, Gaudium et Spes ("Joy and Hope") outlines the pastoral mission of the Church in the modern world. It calls upon all Christians to work for the development of the whole human family based upon a profound recognition of the dignity of the human person.

Flannery, Austin, editor. Vatican Council II: More Post-Conciliar Documents. Collegeville,

Minn.: The Liturgical Press, 1982.

Abstract: This volume serves as a supplement to Flannery's earlier compendium, Vatican Council II: The Conciliar and Post-Conciliar Documents, which was published in 1975. In this volume the editor collects official Church documents issued since 1975 that relate to the major conciliar documents.

 

———, editor. Vatican Council II: The Conciliar and Post-Conciliar Documents. Wilmington,

Del.: Scholarly Resources, 1975.

Abstract: This one volume compendium reprints all of the sixteen documents of the Second Vatican Council and a selection of subsequent official documents associated with them. The major documents of Vatican II are available on the web at http://www.christusrex.org/www1/CDHN/v1.html.
