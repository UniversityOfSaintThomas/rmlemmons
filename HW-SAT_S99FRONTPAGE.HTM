TAKE-HOME FINAL EXAM:  On May 15th, hand in a short paper (approximately 2 pages long). In this paper defend an answer to one of the following questions:  (1) Do human beings have immaterial souls? (2) Do human beings have immortal souls? (3) What is the most important thing for human beings to accomplish with their lives? (4) Is the nature of human beings different from the natures of the other animals and computers?  (5) Is American culture supportive of the mission of human beings? The defense of your answer must include a persuasive argument that your answer is right and that the arguments against your position are mistaken.
