I: BASIC PHILOSOPHIES OF PERSON



A: DUALISM (PLATONIC AND CARTESIAN): THERE IS ONLY ONE TYPE OF SOUL AND IT IS IMMORTAL

(1) This soul belongs to all living things (P)

(2) This soul belongs only to human beings (D)

(3) The body is an organic machine (D)

(4) Life's goal: the rule of reason and virtue



B: HYLOMORPHISM: THERE ARE THREE DIFFERENT TYPES OF SOUL

(1) Human souls have the most immateriality; vegetative souls have the least; and sentient souls are intermediary. (A)

(2) Higher souls can do all that the lower souls can do. (A)

(3) Only the human soul is immortal. (A, D)

(4) The human body is the result of the soul's animation of matter. (A)

(5) The human soul cannot know without the bodily senses and its images in this life. (A)

(6) Every soul unifies all parts into one being. (A)

(7) Life's goal: Contemplating God and Living Virtuously With Love (A)



C: PHILOSOPHICAL BUDDHISM: THERE IS NO SOUL OR UNIFYING PRINCIPLE FOR THE FIVE AGGREGATES

(1) Reincarnation without transmigration for the immoral

(2) Life's goal: Live with compassion and with the wisdom in order to end one's existence and the cycle of karmic reincarnations.



D: MATERIALISM

(1) In no way does the immaterial exist. (S, DE)

(2) Human behavior is determined; there is no free will

(3) Matter and its properties explain all. (E, S, DE)

(4) Life's goal: maximize pleasure and avoid pain



E: MOST BASIC OPTIONS
