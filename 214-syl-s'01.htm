DESCRIPTION:

This course has two goals. The first goal is to identify through philosophical argument the ethics that best respects human dignity. The second is to apply that ethics to some of the difficult issues confronting the 21st century, e.g. euthanasia, warfare, capital punishment, abortion, cloning human beings etcetera.
