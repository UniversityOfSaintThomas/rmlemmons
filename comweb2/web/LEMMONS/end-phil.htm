PL 430: THE END OF PHILOSOPHY?

Given the critiques of postmodernism and feminism,, is it possible for philosophy to continue? Must philosophy end, or can it be saved? If so, how? This senior seminar confronts the various ideas of postmodernism and seeks  to determine what would enable philosophy to endure into the next century.

TEXTS: Reason, Truth and Self, by Michael Luntley; The Truth About Truth, Edited by Walter Truett Anderson
