CULTUAL ARTIFACT PRESENTATION
 

Present to the class some aspect of American culture that reflects a theme of the course. Include in your presentation at least one argument that your artifact reflects both American culture and a theme of the course. Please provide the class with a copy of your argument.

VCRs and cassette players will be available.

N.B. If you wish to argue that American culture is materialistic, then your artifact must illustrate a necessary aspect of materialism, or the supremacy of materialistic values.
