". . .God is love. In this way, the love of God was revealed to us: God sent His only Son into the world so that we might have life through Him. In this is love: not that we have loved God, but that He loved us . . ."
1 John 4:8-10
 

"It is Christ, the last Adam, who fully discloses man to himself and unfolds his noble calling by revealing the mystery of the Father and the Father’s love." Gaudium et Spes, 22.
 

Thus, in revealing man to himself, Christ reveals that man is made for love, i.e. to cherish and to care for others. 
 

"For this is the message you have heard from the beginning: we should love one another, . . ."
1 John 3:11.
 

The loving heart beats with the joys of heaven.
