CSMA 593.02: Faith and Politics–Controversies

Dr. Lemmons

Spring 2006



What role should faith play in politics? Does the First Amendment require a state-sponsored secularism? Can the American system survive without a consensus on basic values? What is the best basis for building consensus? What are the political responsibilities of the faithful Catholic? These and related issues shall be explored within the context of the American Constitution and Catholic socio-political philosophy.
